Human-Resource-Dashboard

This project shows the trend of hires and exits in an organization. It also breaks down the payment structure of each department in the company including basic pay, allowances, tax, and cost to the employer. It tracks employees' performance, period of hire and cause of exit for every gender. This helps the HR department have an holistic access to the performance of a staff and can easily decide on promotion or other compensations.
